websrvr

Deploy your websites to Cloudfront/Amazon S3 using dropbox.

1. Authorize websrvr

This creates an app folder in your dropbox to which we have access. It doesn't give us access to your full dropbox.

»

2. Create a folder

Now, create a folder in your Home » Dropbox » Apps » websrvr, and put your html / css / js / images in it to publish the site.

»

3. Go online

You can now go online to the folder-name.websrvr.in to view your website online. Edit your files locally to updated the site.
